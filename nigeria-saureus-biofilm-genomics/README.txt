Supplementary materials for: "Genomic profiling of biofilm‑associated genes in clinical Staphylococcus aureus from Nigeria: association with multidrug resistance and clonal lineages"

Contents:
- Supplementary_Table_S1_metadata.csv: Clinical metadata (age, sex, sample source, hospital, diagnosis) for 107 isolates.
- Supplementary_Table_S2_Dunn_test.csv: Pairwise Dunn's post‑hoc comparisons for biofilm score by MLST (Bonferroni‑adjusted p‑values).
- Supplementary_Table_S3_correlations.csv: Spearman correlations between biofilm score and AMR functional classes.
- Supplementary_Table_S4_Youden.csv: Optimal threshold, sensitivity, specificity, and accuracy for predicting MRSA using biofilm score.
- Supplementary_Table_S4_confusion_matrix.csv: 2×2 confusion matrix at the Youden cut‑off.
- Supplementary_Table_S5_Firth.csv: Full output of Firth's penalised logistic regression (biofilm_score + MLST).
- Supplementary_Figure_S5.pdf: Heatmap of biofilm gene presence/absence for major STs (≥5 isolates).
- Raw data CSV files: amr_genes.csv, virulence_genes.csv, sample_overview.csv, bacmet_genes.csv, biofilm_analysis_dataset.csv.
- All R scripts (*.R) used for statistical analysis and figure generation.

All other files are also available from the corresponding authors upon request.
